About This Project

HaruWine is an AI-powered cocktail sommelier that understands context. Unlike traditional recipe apps, it considers your current mood, the weather outside, time of day, and the occasion to recommend the perfect drink for your moment.

The application features a beautiful, intuitive interface with support for multiple languages (Chinese, Japanese, English), user authentication, favorites system, and a curated cocktail library organized by style.
